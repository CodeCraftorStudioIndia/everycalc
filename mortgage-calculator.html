How to Use This Calculator

Complete mortgage analysis in 6 precise steps:

  1. Property Price: Total agreement value including registration/stamp duty.
  2. Down Payment: 10-20% typical. Banks require minimum 10-20%.
  3. Interest Rate: Current home loan rates 8.4-9.2%. Women get 0.05-0.1% extra.
  4. Loan Tenure: 15-30 years standard. Shorter = less interest, higher EMI.
  5. Tax Bracket: Your marginal tax rate. New regime has limited deductions.
  6. Click Calculate: See EMI, tax savings, amortization + affordability analysis.

Pro Tips: Down payment >20% gets better rates. Pre-approve from 3 banks. Factor 1-2% processing fees + 5-7% stamp duty.

Understanding Your Mortgage Results

Key outputs explained:

  • Monthly EMI: Fixed payment covering principal + interest.
  • Total Interest: 70-120% of loan amount over tenure.
  • Tax Savings: Section 80C (₹1.5L principal) + 24(b) (₹2L interest). 30% bracket = ₹1.05L annual savings.
  • Effective Cost: Total interest - tax benefits over tenure.

  • Amortization: Early payments = 70-90% interest. Year 10+ shifts to principal.

Decision Framework: EMI <35% take-home pay = comfortable. 35-50% = manageable. >50% = risky unless dual income.

Mortgage Formula & Tax Benefits

EMI Formula: P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)

Tax Benefits (Old Regime):

  • Section 80C: Max ₹1.5L principal repayment annually
  • Section 24(b): Max ₹2L home loan interest annually
  • Total deduction: ₹3.5L → ₹1.05L tax savings (30% bracket)

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Down payment mandatory?
Yes. Banks require 10-20% minimum. >20% = better rates + lower EMI.

2. New vs old tax regime?
Old regime needed for deductions. New regime: no 80C/24b benefits but lower rates.

3. Can EMI change?
Floating rate (99% loans): Yes, with RBI repo changes. Fixed rate: No.

4. Prepayment rules?
Floating loans: Unlimited free prepayments. Fixed: 2-4% charge on amount prepaid.

5. Processing fees?
0.25-1% of loan. Negotiable. Existing customers get waivers.

6. Women borrowers benefit?
Yes. 0.05-0.15% lower rates from most banks + priority processing.

7. Joint loan advantages?
Combined income → higher eligibility. Both get tax benefits on shared ownership.

8. Balance transfer worth it?
Yes if rate drops >0.5% + processing waiver. Saves ₹8-12L on ₹50L loans.

Home Loan Strategies

Prepayment Priority: Years 1-7 maximum impact. Use bonuses, maturity proceeds.

Rate Shopping: Get 3 pre-approvals. Match lowest rate + 0.1% processing waiver.

Tenure Sweet Spot: EMI comfortable + interest minimized. Usually 20 years.

Documentation: Ready: 6 months salary slips/statements, 3 ITRs, property papers.
